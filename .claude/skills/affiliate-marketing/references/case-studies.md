# Case Studies

## Legendary Success Stories

### Dropbox (3900% Growth)
- **Reward:** 500MB storage per referral (both parties)
- **Integration:** Onboarding step 6
- **Key:** Marginal cost, massive perceived value

### PayPal (100M Users)
- **Incentive:** $20+$20 cash
- **Growth:** 7-10% daily
- **Key:** Aggressive incentives for high-LTV products

### Airbnb (900% YoY)
- **Strategy:** Altruistic messaging outperformed selfish
- **Focus:** Mobile-first optimization
- **Key:** A/B test messaging continuously

## What Doesn't Work

| Anti-Pattern | Fix |
|--------------|-----|
| Low commission (<15%) | Increase to 25-30% |
| No support | Monthly check-ins |
| Auto-approval | Manual vetting |
| One-time only | Recurring commissions |
