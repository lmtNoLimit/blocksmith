# Compliance & Legal

## FTC Disclosure (US)

### Requirements
- **Conspicuous:** Large, high contrast
- **Clear Language:** "Ad", "#sponsored", "#ad"
- **Placement:** Near affiliate link, not hidden
- **Proximity:** Adjacent to promotional content

### Approved: "Ad", "Sponsored", "#ad", "I earn commission"
### Prohibited: "sp", "collab", "partner", hidden disclosures

### Penalties: Up to $51,744 per violation, joint liability

## GDPR (EU/EEA)

## Contract Essentials

### Commission Structure
```
Commission: [X]% of subscription revenue
Cookie: 90 days
Attribution: Last-click
Payment: Net-30, $100 minimum
```

### Disclosure Clause (Mandatory)
```
Affiliate agrees to:
- FTC-compliant disclosure in EVERY post
- Use approved language (#ad, Sponsored)
- Not delete disclosures for 12 months
- Notify Brand of FTC inquiries
```

### Exclusivity (Optional)
```
During Term and 90 days after:
No promoting competing products in [category].
```

### Termination
```
Either party: 30-day written notice
For Cause: Immediate (fraud, violation)
Post-termination: Cease marketing, remove links
```

## Tax Requirements

### US
- Affiliates >$600/year: 1099-NEC required
- Maintain 7-year records

### International
- Withholding varies by country
- EU: VAT for digital services
- Document affiliate residence
