# KOL/KOC Partnerships

## KOL vs KOC Comparison

| Aspect | KOL (Key Opinion Leader) | KOC (Key Opinion Consumer) |
|--------|--------------------------|----------------------------|
| Definition | Authority/expert with large following | Authentic consumer sharing experiences |
| Reach | Large (10K-1M+) | Smaller (1K-100K) |
| Cost | $1,000-$50,000+ per post | Product gifting or small fees |
| Conversion | Moderate | High (92% prefer peer recommendations) |
| Best For | Brand awareness, launches | Affiliate revenue, conversions |

## When to Use Each

**Use KOL If:**
- Launching new category
- Need brand credibility
- Targeting broad market
- Budget $5K+

**Use KOC If:**
- Need conversions now
- Limited budget
- Targeting niche audience
- Want authentic testimonials

## 12-Step Vetting Checklist

**Phase 1: Initial Screening**
1. Audience alignment with ICP
2. Follower authenticity (<15% fake)
3. Engagement quality (2-3% micro, 0.5-2% macro)

**Phase 2: Content Analysis**
4. Past brand partnerships (no conflicts)
5. Content quality and consistency
6. Value alignment with brand

**Phase 3: Risk Assessment**
7. Brand safety checks (controversies)
8. FTC disclosure track record
9. Audience behavior patterns

**Phase 4: Partnership Viability**
10. Response time and professionalism
11. Rate reasonableness
12. Long-term potential

## Compensation Models

| Model | Best For | Range |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Flat Fee | Awareness campaigns | $500-$5,000/post |
| Commission | Performance scaling | 10-40% of revenue |
| Hybrid | Long-term partnerships | $1-3K base + 15-25% |
| Revenue Share | Startup partnerships | 5-20% of LTV |

## Recommended Hybrid Model

```
Base: $2,000/month retainer
Commission: 15% of subscription revenue
Bonus Tiers:
- 10+ customers = 20%
- 20+ customers = 25%
- 50+ customers = 30%
Payment: Net-30
Term: 6 months minimum
```

## Red Flags (Disqualifying)

- Fake followers >15%
- Engagement rate <0.5%
- Competing partnerships
- FTC non-compliance history
- Audience mismatch >30%
