# Content Strategy Framework

## Strategy Components

### 1. Audience Analysis
- Personas with demographics and psychographics
- Pain points and challenges
- Content consumption preferences
- Buyer journey stages

### 2. Content Pillars (3-5 core themes)
- Aligned with business goals
- Address audience needs
- Support keyword strategy
- Enable topic clustering

### 3. Content Types
| Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Blog | SEO, education | 2-4x/week |
| Video | Engagement | 1-2x/week |
| Podcast | Authority | 1x/week |
| Social | Distribution | Daily |
| Email | Nurture | 1-2x/week |

### 4. Distribution Channels
- **Owned:** Blog, email, social profiles
- **Earned:** PR, guest posts, mentions
- **Paid:** Ads, sponsorships, influencers

### 5. Success Metrics
- Traffic (sessions, users)
- Engagement (time on page, shares)
- Conversions (leads, sales)
- SEO (rankings, backlinks)

## Content Pillar Structure

```
Main Pillar (3000+ words)
├── Cluster Article 1 (1500 words)
├── Cluster Article 2 (1500 words)
├── Cluster Article 3 (1500 words)
└── Supporting Content
    ├── FAQ pages
    ├── Glossary terms
    └── Resource lists
```
