# Audio & Music Trends Reference

Music production styles and sonic branding for digital advertising.

## Music Style Quick Reference

| Style | Description | Best For | Keywords |
|-------|-------------|----------|----------|
| **Cinematic Emotional** | Sweeping strings, ambient builds, narrative arc | Brand stories, launches, emotional | epic, orchestral, building, emotional, film |
| **Hybrid Genre Fusion** | EDM + Orchestral + Organic elements | Niche targeting, modern brands | hybrid, fusion, modern, layered, eclectic |
| **Commercial EDM** | Dance music with cinematic depth | Lifestyle, youth, energy/momentum | upbeat, electronic, dance, energetic, driving |
| **Nostalgic Retro** | 80s/90s sounds, familiar callbacks | Heritage, Millennial/Gen X targeting | retro, vintage, throwback, nostalgic, classic |
| **Lo-fi Chill** | Relaxed, bedroom-produced feel | Wellness, Gen Z, casual content | chill, lo-fi, relaxed, mellow, laid-back |
| **Acoustic Organic** | Guitar, piano, natural instruments | Authenticity, warmth, storytelling | acoustic, organic, warm, natural, genuine |
| **Corporate Ambient** | Professional, unobtrusive, productive | B2B, corporate, professional | corporate, ambient, professional, subtle, clean |
| **Trending Sounds** | Platform-popular tracks | TikTok, Reels, viral content | trending, viral, popular, current, platform |

## Sonic Branding Elements

**Audio logo:** 2-5 second signature sound (Netflix "ta-dum", Intel chime)
**Brand anthem:** Full musical piece embodying brand
**UI sounds:** App/product interaction audio
**Hold music:** Telephone/wait experience
**Podcast intro/outro:** Consistent show identity

## Platform Music Considerations

| Platform | Music Note |
|----------|------------|
| TikTok | Trending sounds crucial; hook in 3s; mute-compatible |
| Instagram Reels | Music-driven; licensed tracks; trending audio |
| YouTube | Longer tracks; narrative arc; licensed or royalty-free |
| LinkedIn | Subtle, professional; often none for native feel |
| Stories | Quick hooks; trending; beat-sync to visuals |
| Podcast | Consistent intro/outro; subtle bed under VO |

## Music Selection Checklist

- [ ] Matches brand personality/positioning
- [ ] Appropriate for target demographic
- [ ] Licensed properly (sync license)
- [ ] Hook in first 3-8 seconds for short-form
- [ ] Works with sound off (visual strong alone)
- [ ] Platform-appropriate (trending vs original)
- [ ] Doesn't overshadow message
- [ ] Consistent across campaign assets

## Quick Music Brief Template

```

```

## Trending Music Stats

- 25% of music requests include "cinematic"
- 90% of video marketers prefer short-form (music must hook fast)
- Sync licensing market: $650M (2024)
- Sound-on viewing: varies by platform (TikTok highest)

## Audio Accessibility

- Always provide captions/subtitles
- Design for sound-off viewing first
- Music shouldn't compete with VO
- Consider hearing-impaired audiences
