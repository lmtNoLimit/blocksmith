# Color Psychology in Advertising

Quick reference for color selection in marketing campaigns.

## Impact Stats
- 85% purchasing decisions influenced by color
- 62-90% snap judgments based on color alone
- +80% brand recognition with consistent colors
- 78% recall brand color vs 43% recall brand name

## Color Meanings & Applications

| Color | Emotion | Best For | Caution |
|-------|---------|----------|---------|
| **Red** | Excitement, urgency, passion, power | CTAs, sales, urgency, action brands | Overstimulating if overused |
| **Blue** | Trust, security, calm, intelligence | Finance, tech, SaaS, healthcare | Can feel cold without warm accents |
| **Green** | Growth, nature, balance, health | Eco, wellness, organic, finance (money) | Culture-dependent meanings |
| **Yellow** | Optimism, warmth, happiness, attention | Family brands, attention-grab, warmth | Hard to read, overstimulating |
| **Orange** | Playful, friendly, creative, energy | Creative, youth, friendly brands | Less formal than red/blue |
| **Purple** | Luxury, creativity, royalty, mystery | Premium, creative, spiritual | Can feel juvenile wrong shade |
| **Black** | Luxury, power, sophistication, elegance | Premium, formal, high-end | Needs strategic white space |
| **White** | Clean, pure, simple, fresh | Healthcare, minimalist, tech | Empty without content/accents |
| **Gray** | Professional, neutral, timeless | Corporate, B2B, backgrounds | Uninspiring alone |
| **Pink** | Feminine, nurture, playful, soft | Beauty, feminine, playful | Gender stereotype risk |
| **Brown** | Earthy, warm, stable, natural | Organic, craft, heritage | Heavy, needs lighter accents |
| **Gold/Silver** | Premium, luxury, prestige | Luxury accents, special editions | Overuse feels cheap |

## Combination Strategies

**Complementary** (opposites): Blue+Orange, Red+Green - High energy, visual tension
**Analogous** (adjacent): Blue+Teal+Green - Harmony, sophistication, calm
**Monochromatic** (one hue): Single color tints/shades - Premium, focused, sophisticated
**Triadic** (equally spaced): Red+Yellow+Blue - Balanced, vibrant, playful

## Quick Palette Selector

**Warm palette** (Red, Orange, Yellow, Brown): Energy, action, urgency, friendliness
**Cool palette** (Blue, Green, Purple): Calm, trust, stability, professionalism

## Industry Defaults

| Industry | Expected Colors | Breaking Convention |
|----------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Finance | Blue, navy, gray | Green for growth, gold for premium |
| Tech | Blue, purple, gradients | Black for premium, neon for edgy |
| Health | Green, blue, white | Warm colors for wellness |
| Beauty | Pink, purple, gold | Bold colors for edgy brands |
| Food | Red, yellow, green | Depends on positioning |
| Luxury | Black, gold, white | Monochromatic for modern |
| Organic | Green, brown, cream | Blue for clean/pure angle |

## Context Rule

**Appropriateness > Color psychology.** Does the color fit what's being sold?

Cultural variations:
- Red: Luck (China) vs Danger (Western)
- White: Purity (Western) vs Mourning (some Asian)
- Purple: Royalty (Western) vs Varies globally

## Application Checklist

- [ ] Primary color matches brand personality
- [ ] Accent color provides contrast/hierarchy
- [ ] CTA color stands out from surroundings
- [ ] Text readable on background (WCAG 4.5:1)
- [ ] Consistent palette across all assets
- [ ] Cultural appropriateness verified
