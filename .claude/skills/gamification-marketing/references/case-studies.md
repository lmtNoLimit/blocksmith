# Case Studies

## Duolingo - Streak Mastery

**Challenge:** Language learning 95% dropout
**Solution:** Gamification-first approach

| Metric | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| 7-day Retention | +3.6x with streak |
| Churn Reduction | 21% with streak freeze |
| DAU Growth | 350% over time |

**Key Mechanics:** Streaks, hearts/lives, leagues, gems, progress bars
**Lessons:** Streaks = habit loops; Loss aversion monetizes well

## Starbucks Rewards - Tier Excellence

**Challenge:** Coffee commoditized, low switching cost
**Solution:** Points + tiers + personalization

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Members | 32M+ US active |
| Revenue Share | 40% of transactions |
| Retention | 44% vs 25% non-members |

**Structure:** Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum (2 Stars/$1)
**Lessons:** Simple earning drives adoption; Tiers create aspiration

---

## Nike Run Club - Community + Commerce

**Challenge:** Fitness apps commoditized
**Solution:** Community-driven gamification

**Key Mechanics:** Challenges, streaks, achievements, leaderboards, guided runs
**Integration:** Engagement → Achievement → Social Share → Commerce Upsell
**Lessons:** Social amplifies achievements; Content adds value

## Anti-Patterns

- **Zynga:** Pure black hat = high churn. Balance 70/30.
- **Gamification Washing:** Points without psychology = ignored
- **Over-Complexity:** Too many currencies = abandonment
