# Engagement Templates

## Engagement Hook Types

### Questions
- "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
- "How do you handle [situation]?"
- "What would you do if [scenario]?"
- "Which [option] do you prefer and why?"

### Polls/Surveys
- "Quick poll: [Option A] or [Option B]?"
- "What's your priority this [timeframe]?"
- "Rate your [skill] from 1-10"

### This or That
- "[Option A] or [Option B]?"
- "Early bird or night owl?"
- "[Tool A] vs [Tool B]?"

### Fill in the Blank
- "The best advice I ever received was _____"
- "I wish I knew _____ when I started [activity]"
- "My unpopular opinion about [topic] is _____"

### Hot Takes
- "Unpopular opinion: [statement]"
- "Hot take: [controversial stance]"
- "Agree or disagree: [statement]"

## Response Templates

### Positive Engagement
- "Great point! What made you think of that?"
- "This is exactly right. I'd add [insight]"
- "Love this perspective. Have you tried [suggestion]?"

### Adding Value
- "Here's a resource that might help: [link]"
- "I wrote about this recently: [link]"
- "Quick tip: [actionable advice]"

### Starting Conversations
- "Interesting! Tell me more about [aspect]"
- "How did you get into [topic]?"
- "What's your experience with this?"

## Community Building

### Acknowledgment
- Celebrate wins: "Congrats on [achievement]!"
- Show appreciation: "Thanks for sharing this!"
- Welcome newcomers: "Great to have you here!"

### Collaboration Hooks
- "Looking for [type of person] to [collaborate on]"
- "Who wants to do a [challenge/project] together?"
- "Tag someone who needs to see this"
